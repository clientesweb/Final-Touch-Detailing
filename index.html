Frequently Asked Questions

What is mobile detailing?

Mobile detailing is a service where we come to your location to clean and detail your vehicle. This saves you time and provides convenience, as you don't need to leave your home or office.

How long does a detailing service take?

The duration of a detailing service can vary depending on the size of your vehicle and the level of detailing required. On average, a full detailing service can take anywhere from 2 to 5 hours.

What's included in your basic detailing package?

Our basic detailing package typically includes exterior wash and dry, interior vacuuming, window cleaning, tire dressing, and a basic wipe-down of interior surfaces. For a full list of services, please contact us.

How often should I have my car detailed?

For optimal results, we recommend having your car detailed every 4-6 months. However, this can vary based on your driving habits, where you park your car, and local weather conditions.

What is ceramic coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that is applied to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection that enhances gloss and provides long-lasting protection against environmental contaminants.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer gift certificates for all of our services. These make great gifts for car enthusiasts or anyone who takes pride in their vehicle. Please contact us for more information on purchasing a gift certificate.
